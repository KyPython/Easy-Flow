## Customization Notes for Sales Team

### Before Sending, Customize These Sections:

1. **Subject Line:** Use Option 1 if you know their specific pain point. Use Option 2 for generic/first calls.

2. **Opening Paragraph:** Replace `[SPECIFIC_PAIN_POINT]` with THEIR language:
   - ‚ùå "manual data entry" (generic)
   - ‚úÖ "copying client info from 5 spreadsheets into Asana every Monday" (specific)

3. **ROI Calculation:**
   - Use their actual numbers from discovery
   - Formula: `(Hours Saved √ó Their Hourly Rate) - $226 (first month cost) = Net Savings`
   - Example: "(20 hours √ó $50/hr) - $226 = $774 saved in month 1"

4. **What You Get (Setup Fee):**
   - List their actual integrations (not generic ones)
   - Reference their use case specifically

5. **Call Agenda ‚Äì Section 2:**
   - Replace `[SPECIFIC_PAIN_POINT]` with their workflow
   - Example: "your client onboarding process" or "your weekly reporting nightmare"

6. **What to Prepare:**
   - Add or remove checklist items based on their tech stack
   - If they mentioned specific tools, list them by name

7. **P.S. Section:**
   - Link to relevant case study or demo if available
   - OR remove entirely if no relevant example exists

## Timing Guide for Different Time Zones

When scheduling, always confirm THEIR timezone:

```
"Our call is [CALL_DATE] at [CALL_TIME] YOUR TIME (that's [YOUR_TIME] for me)."
```

Use: `9am PST / 12pm EST / 5pm GMT` format if multiple stakeholders.

## Red Flags to Watch For (Adjust Template If You See These)

‚ùå **They haven't described their workflow in detail yet**  
‚Üí Send discovery questions email FIRST, then this template

‚ùå **They're comparing you to Zapier/Make**  
‚Üí Emphasize custom builds vs. DIY, add case study comparison

‚ùå **They want to "think about it"**  
‚Üí Shorten call to 30 min, remove pricing table, focus on demo only

‚ùå **Budget concerns**  
‚Üí Emphasize ROI harder, offer payment plan: "$197 setup + first month free trial"

## Success Metrics

Track these after each email sent:
- **Open rate:** Target 70%+ (use personalized subject lines)
- **Response rate:** Target 60%+ (strong CTA + urgency)
- **Show-up rate:** Target 80%+ (reminder + clear value prop)

If any metric is low, A/B test:
- Subject lines
- Call length (try 30 min vs 60 min)
- Pricing visibility (show vs hide until call)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Using generic placeholders ("your business process")  
‚ùå Forgetting to calculate/show ROI  
‚ùå Making the call sound like a sales pitch (it's a working session)  
‚ùå Not confirming their timezone  
‚ùå Sending without a clear CTA

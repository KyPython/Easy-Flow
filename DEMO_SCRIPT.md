## üé¨ Demo Flow

### **PART 1: The Problem (30 seconds)**

**"Let me show you what EasyFlow does"**

1. **Open Landing Page** (`/`)
   - Point to hero section: "Stop Doing Boring Work"
   - Highlight the "Before vs After" message
   - "This is the problem - people spend hours doing repetitive tasks that could be automated"

2. **Show Value Propositions**
   - Scroll to feature cards:
     - **"Send Welcome Emails Automatically"** - Before: 15 min per customer ‚Üí After: Instant
     - **"Create Weekly Reports"** - Before: 2 hours ‚Üí After: One click
     - **"Update CRM from Forms"** - Before: 45 min daily ‚Üí After: Automatic

**Key Message**: "These are real problems people face every day. EasyFlow solves them."

---

### **PART 2: Live Automation Demo (2-3 minutes)**

**"Let me show you how it works"**

#### Step 1: Demo Portal (1 minute)
1. **Navigate to Demo Portal** (`/demo`)
   - "We have a demo portal that simulates a real business scenario"
   - Login with: `demo@useeasyflow.com` / `demo123`
   - Show invoices list
   - "Normally, someone would manually log in here every day to download invoices"

2. **Switch to Dashboard**
   - "With EasyFlow, we can automate this entire process"
   - Go to `/app` (logged in)

#### Step 2: Quick Task Creation (1-2 minutes)
1. **Go to Task Management** (`/app/tasks`)
   - "Let me show you how easy it is to automate this"

2. **Create a Simple Automation Task**
   - Click "New Task" or use the form
   - Demo: Scrape a website (e.g., news headlines)
   - **Alternative**: Show existing demo task that's already configured
   - Submit the task

3. **Show Execution**
   - Wait for task to process (should be quick)
   - Show results when complete
   - Highlight: "This just saved someone 10-15 minutes of manual work"

**Key Message**: "See how simple that was? No coding, no complex setup. Just describe what you want."

---

### **PART 3: AI Workflow Agent (2 minutes)**

**"But it gets even better - we have an AI assistant"**

1. **Open AI Workflow Agent** (click the AI icon in bottom right)
   - "This is our AI assistant - you can just talk to it"

2. **Demonstrate Natural Language Workflow Creation**
   - Say something like: *"Monitor Hacker News headlines daily and email me the top 5"*
   - Or: *"Create a workflow that scrapes product prices from example.com"*
   - Show how it understands and creates the workflow automatically

3. **Show Generated Workflow**
   - Navigate to Workflows page (`/app/workflows`)
   - Show the workflow that was just created
   - Explain: "The AI built this entire workflow for you based on just one sentence"

**Key Message**: "You don't need to know how to build workflows - just tell our AI what you want."

---

### **PART 4: Workflow Builder (1 minute)**

**"For more complex automations, we have a visual builder"**

1. **Navigate to Workflow Builder** (`/app/workflows`)
   - Show the visual canvas
   - "You can build multi-step automations with a drag-and-drop interface"

2. **Show Example Workflow** (if one exists)
   - Point to different steps: Start ‚Üí Scrape ‚Üí Transform ‚Üí Email
   - "Each step does something specific, and you can chain them together"

3. **Demonstrate Templates** (if available)
   - "We also have pre-built templates for common workflows"
   - Show template gallery if available

**Key Message**: "From simple tasks to complex multi-step workflows - we support everything."

---

### **PART 5: Value Summary (30 seconds)**

**"Let me show you what this means for your business"**

1. **Show History/Results** (`/app/history`)
   - "Here's all the automations that have run"
   - Show time saved, success rates
   - "This is real time and money saved"

2. **Quick Stats**
   - Point out any analytics showing:
     - Tasks completed
     - Time saved
     - Success rate

**Key Message**: "This is the ROI - hours saved, fewer errors, more focus on important work."

---

### **PART 6: Closing & CTA (30 seconds)**

1. **Return to Landing Page**
   - "EasyFlow turns hours of repetitive work into seconds of automation"
   - "No coding required - just describe what you want"

2. **Call to Action**
   - Point to CTA button: "Book a 20-min Workflow Setup Call"
   - "We'll help you identify your biggest time-wasters and automate them for you"
   - "Free for early users - no credit card required"

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Demo Tips

1. **Keep it conversational** - Don't read from script, make it natural
2. **Show, don't tell** - Actually run the automation, don't just describe it
3. **Handle errors gracefully** - If something doesn't work, acknowledge it and move on
4. **Focus on value** - Always tie features back to time/money saved
5. **End with CTA** - Always guide them to book a call or sign up
